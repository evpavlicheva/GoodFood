# GoodFood — Product Overview

## What it is

GoodFood is a playful, Duolingo-style web app that turns healthy eating into a game kids actually want to play. A friendly fruit-and-veggie mascot guides each child through browsing a menu, building an order, and tracking it — while parents get a simple dashboard to manage the menu and see what's being ordered.

## Core value for kids

- **A buddy, not a chore.** Kids pick a mascot (Brocco, Carrie, Appy, and friends) who cheers them on, reacts to their choices, and makes food feel like an adventure instead of a lecture.
- **Independence with guardrails.** Children can browse the menu, add dishes to their cart, and place orders themselves — building autonomy and decision-making skills in a safe, closed environment.
- **Instant, joyful feedback.** Smooth animations, encouraging messages, and a "ready to pick up" status turn ordering food into a small win every time.
- **Made for them, not adapted for them.** Big touch targets, simple language (with full English/Russian support), bright colors, and zero clutter — designed for how kids actually use phones and tablets.
- **Their own space on a shared device.** Multiple kids in one household can each have their own profile, mascot, and order history — switching takes one tap.

## Core value for parents

- **Full control over the menu.** Parents (in Parent Mode) add dishes with photos, descriptions, and nutrition info — and AI-assisted photo analysis fills in calories, protein, fat, carbs, and fun facts automatically.
- **Visibility without micromanaging.** A live orders dashboard shows what each child ordered and its status, so parents stay informed without hovering.
- **Availability control.** Mark a dish "unavailable" and it's instantly greyed out and unselectable in the kids' menu — no awkward "sorry, we're out of that" moments.
- **Healthy habits, encouraged not enforced.** By curating the menu themselves, parents shape what's possible to choose — kids still feel they're choosing freely.
- **Works the way families actually live.** One shared tablet, multiple kids, two languages, simple PIN-protected parent access — no accounts, no friction.

## In one sentence

GoodFood makes choosing healthy food feel like a game kids want to play, while giving parents an easy way to shape the menu and keep an eye on what's happening — without nagging, spreadsheets, or extra apps.
